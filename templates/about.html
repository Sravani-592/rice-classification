About Our Project

Understanding the world of rice, one grain at a time.

Project Aim

Our goal is to leverage the power of deep learning to create an accessible, user-friendly tool for identifying various rice varieties. This project aids farmers, researchers, and consumers in distinguishing types like Arborio, Basmati, and Jasmine with high accuracy, simply by uploading a picture.

Technology Used

This application is built with a Python Flask backend to serve a powerful TensorFlow/Keras model. The frontend is crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enhanced with the Bootstrap framework for a responsive and modern user interface.

Discover Rice Varieties

Basmati Popular

A long, slender-grained aromatic rice traditionally from the Indian subcontinent.

Jasmine Fragrant

A long-grain variety of fragrant rice with a slightly sticky texture when cooked.

Ipsala Soft Texture

A Turkish-grown rice variety known for its soft texture and consistent grain quality.

Karacadag Ancient Grain

An ancient variety of rice cultivated in Turkey, known for its resilience and aroma.

Arborio Risotto Rice

An Italian short-grain rice, known for its high starch content, perfect for risotto.
